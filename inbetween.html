In Between

2023

I chose to showcase my collection of images that displayed the inbetween by highlighting the CMYK printing method and layers. The K layer is only applied to the text pages, with another layer of separation in itself. I took the meaning of "in-between" to mean "in the middle of."

The nature of the book is cyclical, so while there is technically a title page to start at, the intention behind the wire-bound book is that there is no definitive place of a beginning and end.
