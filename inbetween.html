In Between


Book

2023

I chose to showcase my collection of images that displayed the in between by highlighting the in between of printing process: CMYK layers. The K layer is only applied to the text pages, with another layer of separation in itself. I took the meaning of "in-between" to mean "in the middle of."


The nature of the book is cyclical, so while there is technically a title page to start at, the intention behind the wire-bound book is that there is no definitive place of a beginning and end.
