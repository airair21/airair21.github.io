The Eras Tour:
Taylor Swift

2024


I redesigned Taylor Swift's The Eras Tour with an emphasis for Swift's authorship and penmanship over her own songs and the distinct colors of every era.


The socks are one line from a song from each era's album



The booklet follows Swift's setlist (minus her surprise songs), as well as articles about her personified album eras and the records her world tour has broken.
