Time

2023

This project is all about how time feels and the perception of its "speed". It goes through the course of 2023 and how busy I was every day of the year, including the uncertain and unwritten future.

The hours of the day on the bottom of the spreads change depending on how long those hours felt. On the left axis is the date, so if more days fill the spread from top to bottom then they went by quicker, and vice versa. There are several instances of a day taking up to three spreads long, and one instance of three weeks being cramped into one spread. Whereever there is text and images are the times that I was occupied and how busy I felt.
