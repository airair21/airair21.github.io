Ode to Fanfiction


Cover Design - Book Design - Layout

2023

This book was created from my love of fanfiction, featuring five articles about the topic to help people not in the fandom-know understand. I took heavy inspiration from how Archive of Our Own (AO3) is formatted and tried to translate reading on a screen to a physical page.


This project is dedicated to my dear friend Cat, who introduced me to the wonderful world of fanfictions and fandom interactions.
